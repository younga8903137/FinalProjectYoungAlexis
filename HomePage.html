Pit Bulls, also affectionately known as bully breeds, are probably one of the most misunderstood breeds of our time. These loveable pups have gotten a bad rap over the years but with more breed awareness, we can show the world just how great these breeds can be.


Pit Bulls go by many names: American Pit Bull Terrier, American staffordshire Terrier, and American Bully are just to name a few. The American Bulldog is also grouped in the same category. In fact, there is no specific "pitbull" breed. The term is used to describe dogs with similar traits and characteristics.
